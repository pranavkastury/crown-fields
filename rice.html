Indian Rice Basmati

We can save you from the agony of choice when you are standing in a store and do not know which Rice to choose? Which one is high quality, tasty, easy to prepare? We have already made this choice for you! You just have to place an order.

NATURAL INDIAN RICE BASMATI


It is called the King of Rice because of its unrivaled aroma and taste. Basmati rice is crumbly and very easy to prepare! Such rice is characterized by the fact that it practically does not stick together during cooking. This makes it ideal for side dishes. It is also widely used for cooking pilaf, meat, fish and poultry dishes. Basmati rice is an environmentally friendly product, that is, it does not accumulate salts of heavy metals and other harmful substances. Beneficial features: Basmati contains such useful substances as: dietary fiber, starch, 18 amino acids, mineral compounds!
